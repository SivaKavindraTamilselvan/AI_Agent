================================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================
The Fashion E-Commerce Platform is an online shopping platform dedicated to fashion products, catering to Men, Women, Kids, and Unisex categories. The platform will support various user roles, including customers, vendors, and admins, each with distinct functionalities. The system aims to provide a seamless shopping experience for customers while enabling vendors to showcase their products and admins to manage the platform efficiently.

================================================================
FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================
1. Customer registration and login functionality
2. Product browsing and searching with filtering options (e.g., by category, price, brand)
3. Product details page with size and colour variant options
4. Shopping cart with add, update, remove, and quantity management features
5. Coupon code application in the shopping cart
6. Favourites page for saving and managing products
7. Vendor registration and product upload functionality
8. Admin approval process for vendor-uploaded products
9. Admin roles with specific permissions (product approvals, catalogue management, coupon management, user/vendor management, reports)
10. Coupon creation and management by admins
11. Order management with payment processing integration
12. User account management (customer and vendor profiles)

================================================================
NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================
1. Performance: The platform should handle a minimum of 1000 concurrent users without significant slowdown
2. Security: The system must comply with standard e-commerce security protocols (e.g., SSL, encryption) to protect user data
3. Scalability: The platform should be able to scale up to 10,000 products and 100 vendors without performance issues
4. Usability: The platform should have an intuitive and user-friendly interface for customers, vendors, and admins
5. Availability: The platform should have a minimum uptime of 99.5% per month
6. Compliance: The platform must comply with relevant e-commerce laws and regulations (e.g., GDPR, consumer protection)

================================================================

2. Risk: Security breaches | Likelihood: High | Impact: Critical | Mitigation: Implement robust security measures (e.g., encryption, firewalls) and regular security audits
3. Risk: Performance issues during peak traffic | Likelihood: Medium | Impact: High | Mitigation: Implement load balancing, caching, and content delivery networks (CDNs)
4. Risk: Admin role confusion or misuse | Likelihood: Low | Impact: Medium | Mitigation: Implement clear role definitions, permissions, and access controls
5. Risk: Coupon abuse or exploitation | Likelihood: Medium | Impact: Medium | Mitigation: Implement coupon usage limits, expiry dates, and monitoring
