PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================
The Fashion E-Commerce Platform is an online marketplace dedicated to fashion products, catering to Men, Women, Kids, and Unisex categories. The platform will support various user roles, including customers, vendors, and admins, each with distinct responsibilities and privileges. The system aims to provide a seamless shopping experience for customers while enabling vendors to showcase their products and admins to manage the platform efficiently.

================================================================
FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================
1. Customer registration and account management
2. Product browsing and searching with filtering options (e.g., category, size, colour)
3. Shopping cart management (add, update, remove items, apply coupons)
4. Favourites/wishlist page for customers to save and manage products
5. Vendor registration and product upload with admin approval process
6. Admin roles with split responsibilities (product approvals, category management, coupon management, user/vendor management, reports)
7. Coupon creation and management with expiry dates and usage limits
8. Order total calculation with coupon application
9. Product variant management (size, colour)
10. User account and vendor account management

================================================================
NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================
1. Performance: The platform should handle a minimum of 1000 concurrent users without significant performance degradation
2. Security: The system must comply with standard e-commerce security protocols (e.g., HTTPS, secure payment gateways)
3. Scalability: The platform should be able to scale up to 10,000 products and 100 vendors without compromising performance
4. Usability: The platform should have an intuitive user interface for customers, vendors, and admins
5. Availability: The platform should have a minimum uptime of 99.5%
6. Compliance: The system must comply with relevant e-commerce laws and regulations (e.g., data protection, consumer rights)
