================================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================
The Fashion E-Commerce Platform is a multi-vendor online marketplace where vendors can sell clothing, accessories, and fashion items to customers across all gender categories and kids. The platform aims to provide a seamless and modern shopping experience for customers, while enabling vendors to manage their products and sales effectively. The platform will be used by three primary user roles: Customer, Vendor, and Admin, each with distinct features and functionalities.

================================================================
FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================
1. Customer registration and login with email verification and password recovery
2. Vendor registration and login with business document verification and admin approval
3. Admin login with role-based access control and sub-admin account management
4. Product catalogue with categories, sub-categories, and product attributes
5. Product visibility rules based on admin approval and stock quantity
6. Vendor product management with upload, edit, and deletion capabilities
7. Admin product approval workflow with pending submission queue and rejection reasons
8. Customer browse and search functionality with filters and sorting options
9. Cart page with add, update, and remove item capabilities, and coupon application
10. Favourites/Wishlist page with add, remove, and 'Add to Cart' functionality
11. Checkout and orders with payment method selection, coupon application, and order status tracking
12. Coupon management with creation, edit, and deletion capabilities, and usage rules
13. Admin role details with specific permissions and access control

================================================================
NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================
1. Performance: product listing pages must load within 2 seconds for up to 1,000 concurrent users
2. Security: all passwords stored as hashed values, JWT-based session management, and HTTPS enforced across all pages
3. Scalability: architecture must support horizontal scaling of application servers and database design must support at least 100,000 product SKUs and 500,000 registered users
4. Usability: fully responsive design, accessibility compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA, and intuitive navigation
5. Availability: target uptime of 99.9% excluding scheduled maintenance, with 48 hours advance notice for maintenance

ASSUMPTIONS
================================================================
1. The platform will be hosted on a cloud provider (AWS/Azure/GCP)
2. All monetary values are in Indian Rupees (INR) by default, with currency configuration possible in future
3. SMS OTP service and email service (SMTP or API) will be provided/configured by the client
4. Payment gateway integration (Razorpay/PayU/Stripe) will be confirmed by client before development begins
5. Vendor document verification is manual by User Management Admin in v1.0, with automated KYC verification as a future enhancement
